# Lambda (ƛ)

Hello reader,

Lambda - shortly written as the Greek letter ƛ - is a screenwriting software built on top of the standards for the [Fountain](https://fountain.io) markup rules.
Beyond that, ƛ uses [TipTap](https://tiptap.dev) as the driver, for the lack of a better word, which powers the editor. I built a [React Fountain](https://github.com/mrlemoos/react-fountain) app to validate the creation of the logic and scaffolding of an editor that combines TipTap and the Fountain format as a proof of concept for what now is ƛ.

This project is a love letter for this art that, in my opinion, is at the lowest of its days.

From a screenwriter who felt in love with storytelling at the age of eight who pays his bills with software engineering.
